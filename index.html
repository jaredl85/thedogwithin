I’m a Seattle-based dog trainer with over 15 years of experience. I’ve worked to train dogs in the entertainment industry and for private clients all over the country. My methods are 100% humane and rooted in positive-reinforcement based training.

My approach to training is a little different. First, I train problem behaviors in the environment in which they occur. If your dog has problems in the car, in the house, or in the store, that is where we will do the training. Second, I teach humans too. During our sessions, I will train you how to communicate with – and teach – your dog more effectively, so that you have a set of tools to continue training your dog long after our sessions are over.
