My Approach

Simply put, my approach to dog training is "positive reinforcement". Dogs learn best when their humans are positive, engaged and patient. The focus of training sessions is on both dog and owner. While I'm teaching the dog manners, helping to modify problem behaviors, overcome phobias, and generally how to be a great dog, I'm also training their humans to effectively communicate and teach them long after our training has come to a close.

Most importantly, I teach humans the skills they will need to continue to teach their dogs using C.P.R. principles: Consistency. Patience. Repeat.
